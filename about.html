This is a personal project of mine to create a simpler transition into working for bestbuy as an employee. Beginning working for bestbuy it was quite easy to get sidetracked as their system for injest of new people was, bluntly, not good enough. Finding out what you needed to do in your first 2 weeks was a pain and referencing back to certain information was next to impossible. That is why I made this application to transition into the workforce easier!

I am a senior at Southern Illinois University and my major is Information Technology. The reason that i chose to use bestbuy in my example is that I have been working there for the past 5 years, and I've loved the experience there however training could use some work. This application is meant to be a proof of concept for bestbuy employees, yet I have absolutely no copyright over any of the information on this page.
