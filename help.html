This page contains a rundown of what each page has and what it can do for you!

  • Main
  • Home:
      The home page is the main landing page of the web application and gives a general rundown of what this
    application is meant to be used for.
  • News:
      The news page contains articles and information that is on release to BestBuy employees
    about information pertaining to their role within the company. This could be anything from information
    about new business plans and partnerships with other companies or something like a newsletter letting
    the employee know of bonuses to be expected this year.
  • Help:
      This is a general navigation guru page to help the user get around the web application and
    will help them with navigation questions. This page will also feature a bug report option and allow the
    user to send in a report or ask a question.
  • About:
      This is a general about page with a little bit of information on what the application is
    meant to do and why it was created. It also features a small section talking about me, the creator
    of the web application.
  • E-Learnign
  • Learning Network:
      This is a landing page to send the user to the BestBuy Learning Network, an external tool to complete
    their certifications and also contains notices of items that need to be completed.
  • Atlas:
      This is a landing page like the learning network page, but specific to Apple training for repairs made
    by Geek Squad and Apple specialists in the store. This page, like the Learning Network page, contains
    notices of eLearning’s to do and special training that may need to be completed.
  • External Ceritificates:
      This page has external certifications for vendors that may only pertain to specific roles within
    a given store, such as those for the Pacific Sales, and Magnolia sections of a store.
  • Policies
  • General Policies and Procedures:
      The general policies and procedures page features all of the documents that a sales representative might want to use and
    will show them what is most often used externally via link.
  • Management:
      This page is designed to give a general flow of who is in charge of what around the store and will
    be useful for employees to see who their supervisor is, who is above them and who they need to
    contact for each kind of issue. Each entry of a person on this page will also have a brief description
    of the supervisor’s role and what they have experience with.
  • Q&A:
      This page is in a list format describing common situations and what the correct course of action
    is depending on the scenario. This page will also allow for a user to submit a question for answering later.
  • Emergency
  • Emergency Procedures:
      This page contains links to content describing policies in the case of emergency, including active shooters,
    robberies and the like. All the articles within this page will also be accessible through general policies
    and procedures but this page is more tailored to emergency situations.
  • Helpline:
      This page contains employee crisis help information including those to report harassment in the workplace,
    counseling options available to the employee, and other similar resources that the employee may need from
    time to time.
