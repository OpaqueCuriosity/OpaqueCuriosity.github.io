This web application is meant to be a tool for new and current BestBuy employees, with the objective of making the transition into the BestBuy family easier! First things first lets go over the pages and what they are meant to do. At the top is the navigation between the different pages of the application. There is the home, the page you are on now, News, E-Learnings, 00318 Policies, Emergency, Help, and About. The E-learnings, Policies, and Emergency buttons all have dropdowns to specify the pages that you can go to, and within these pages are going to be links to the actuial BestBuy documentation if you want to read the Legalese. That being said the News page is pretty self explainitory and the help page is meant to understand this application better. The about page is about the developer, and the process to make this application.
