Priority Number 1: YOU

In emergency situations the first priority is your safety.
There are 3 things you need to keep in mind when in an emergency situation:
1. Run
2. Hide
3. Fight

When faced with an emergency there are three main options laid out for you, you run when you can. Hide when you cant run, and when you cant hide any longer you fight.
